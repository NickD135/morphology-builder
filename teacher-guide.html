Teacher Guide

Everything you need to get Word Labs running in your classroom, step by step.

Start here
1
📝 Create your teacher account

Sign up on the Teacher Login page with your email and password. You'll get a free trial to get started — no credit card required.

2
⚙️ Set up your class

Go to Class Setup, name your class, choose a class code, and add your students. Each student gets a unique 3-letter PIN they'll use to log in.

3
🖨️ Print or display login details

Use the printable login cards or display the QR login page on your classroom screen for easy access. Students won't need to remember any passwords.

4
👋 Students log in

Students enter the class code, select their name, and type their 3-letter PIN. No email or password needed — it's designed for young learners.

5
🔬 Explore the activities

Word Labs has 9 activities covering morphemes, phonemes, syllables, and spelling patterns. Try them yourself first so you know what your students will experience.

6
📊 Read your dashboard

The Teacher Dashboard shows heatmaps, accuracy rates, intervention flags, and individual student profiles. Use it to identify who needs extra support and who's ready to move on.

7
🧬 Use Extension Mode

Toggle Extension Mode on per student from Class Setup to give them harder content. This is ideal for students who are ready for more advanced morphemes and word patterns.

8
📄 Print resources

Visit the Teacher Resources page for printable reference cards and offline activity sheets. Great for homework or when screens aren't available.

9
❓ Get help

Check the FAQ page, submit feedback, or email nick@wordlabs.app. We're always happy to help you get the most out of Word Labs.
