Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Word Labs.

For Teachers

How do I create a class? +
Go to Class Setup from the dashboard, enter a class name and unique class code, then add your students. The system generates PINs automatically.
How do students log in? +
Students go to the Word Labs home page, enter your class code, select their name, and type their 3-letter PIN.
What is Extension Mode? +
Extension Mode gives a student harder words, morphemes and phoneme patterns. Toggle it on per student from Class Setup.
How do I see how my students are going? +
Open the Teacher Dashboard, enter your class password, and you'll see heatmaps, intervention flags, and individual student profiles.
How many students can I add? +
The free plan supports up to 5 students. Standard plan supports 35. Pro is unlimited.
What is the daily limit? +
Free plan students can complete up to 30 questions per day. Paid plans have no limit.
Can I print resources for students? +
Yes — visit the Teacher Resources page for printable offline activity sheets.
How do I use the QR code login? +
From your dashboard, open the QR Login page and display it on your classroom screen. Students scan with their iPad camera.

For Students

How do I log in? +
Ask your teacher for your class code and PIN. Enter the class code on the home page, find your name, and type your PIN.
What do Quarks do? +
Quarks are the currency you earn by answering correctly. Spend them in the Lab Shop to customise your scientist.
What is XP? +
XP is experience points that level up your scientist. You earn XP by answering questions correctly.
Why can't I play anymore today? +
You've reached today's question limit on the free plan. Come back tomorrow or ask your teacher.
How do I change my scientist's look? +
Go to My Scientist from the home page and visit the Lab Shop tab to spend your Quarks.
