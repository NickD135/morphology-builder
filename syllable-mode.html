Syllable rules
1Every syllable has one vowel sound (a, e, i, o, u).
2Split between two consonants: but·ter, hap·py.
3A single consonant between vowels usually goes with the next syllable: o·pen.
4Prefixes and suffixes often form their own syllable: re·play, help·ful.
5Compound words split between their two words: sun·shine.
